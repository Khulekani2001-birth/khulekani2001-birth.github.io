About MiltonLearn

Advancing education through innovation and technology

Our Mission

At MiltonLearn, our mission is to innovate and develop learning technologies and solve problems that will reshape education for the better. We strive to keep students engaged and excited about learning by providing them with cutting-edge technology, advanced tutorial classes and study resources.

The Future

We are committed to the future of teaching and learning using advanced technologies. By developing solutions that make teachers' jobs easier, faster, and more efficient, we aim to transform the educational landscape. Our focus is on creating a seamless integration of technology in the classroom, enhancing both teaching and learning experiences.

Words from the Founder

"When I was in high school, I struggled to perform at peak level in my studies with a notable 13% test 1 mark for IGCSE Additional Mathematics in Form 4/Grade 11.

In 2018, I met my high school Chemistry teacher who eventually became my mentor and helped me see the world from a different lens. Fast forward, end of 2018, for the first time, I attained Best Award in school for Form 5/Grade 12. That test 1 -13% changed to an 84% final exam mark.

I then went on to take CIE AS Level, then attained the Top in The World award for AS Level Mathematics with a 100% final exam mark. This elevated me to pursue a BSc Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Cape Town.

Today, I help students who used to be like me improve their confidence and eventually academic performance by offering tutoring services and building educational technology tools to improve access to high quality education."

~ Milton Ngwenya: Founder and CEO, MiltonLearn
